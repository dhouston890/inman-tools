Real estate investors leaned into the Midwest and Northeast in 2025

Investor home purchases nationwide were nearly flat (+1.9 percent) from the first half of 2025 to the second. But beneath that calm surface, investors pulled back by double digits in Sun Belt markets like Tampa, Las Vegas and Phoenix while ramping up buying in more affordable metros in the Midwest and Northeast.
